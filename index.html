Jesse Pinkman AKA CAP'N COOK

About Jesse Pinkman

Jesse Pinkman AKA CAP'N COOK was a small time meth peddler based out of Albuquerque, New Mexico. At one point his old Highschool chem teacher, Mr. White reaches out to him to get into the game. Together, Jesse Pinkman and Mr. White, turn into the biggest baddest most feared dope manufacturers the U.S has ever seen.
